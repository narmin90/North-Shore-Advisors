About North Shore Advisors

We are a process documentation consultancy based on the North Shore of Long Island, New York.

What we do

We help tri-state area manufacturing and logistics companies document their processes, procedures, and policies so they…

  • Maximize productivity and quality
  • Reduce occupational hazards and legal risk
  • Accelerate training and onboarding

Companies hire us because we…
  • Save time- documenting processes is time consuming. It takes your people away from their primary jobs. We take that burden off your workforce so your people focus on the main effort and not time-consuming extra duties.
  • Save money - hiring full time employees is expensive, time consuming, and a hassle. We are easy to hire and quick to deploy. Once our work is complete, that’s it. No need to pay us expensive benefits or worry about long-term projects.
